INTAKE

Before booking a session at Flow of Miracles I have an intake with you to find out what you are looking for and what I can offer you. You can call me during my consultation hour (business days 7pm-8pm) for an intake or e-mail me. We conclude the intake with an appointment for a healing session including the focus or Reading request including the theme or question. You will receive a confirmation by e-mail with my terms and conditions.

Preparation

I will prepare myself around 24 hours before your session. For healing work, I tune into your energy field, connect to intuitive wisdom and write an affirmation card with your own personal story. This card can be used to reconnect with the energy of the session. For readings I prepare myself to be in an intuitive state of mind to get the best results

Session

During a healing session I work with you to acknowledge where you are at, heal the wounded parts and receive the new level of wholeness. My gifts will tell me what you need and which parts of you are involved. I will ask you to take a step into those parts of yourself and through me you will receive what you need to heal them. If you have chosen a phone or e-mail session, I will guide you through these steps or will do them for you and sent you a report afterwards.

A Reading session means you will receive a report 7 business days after payment. It features the energy around you and the issue/theme, advice about the situation or the answer to your question and guidance for the future. The extensive report is a PDF file including pictures of the cards drawn.

After care

Around 2-3 weeks after a Healing session I do a follow-up and discuss preliminary results. If you are in need of a small touch up, I will do so for free by e-mail. After 6 weeks we will discus the results of your session, you will receive a feedback form and we might consider a new session if needed.

With Reading, after care is often a follow-up if requested to see how the situation went.
